Transpose of a Matrix

Consider a matrix \(M\) and its transpose \(M^T\), where: Original Matrix \(M\): \[ M = \begin{pmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \end{pmatrix} \] Transposed Matrix \(M^T\): \[ M^T = \begin{pmatrix} a & d \\ b & e \\ c & f \end{pmatrix} \] Transposing a matrix involves converting its rows into columns and vice versa. This operation is fundamental in linear algebra for various computations and transformations.
